       -with 'o'||'w'||'e'||'a'||'l'||'p'||'t'||'m'  		for  Ω, ω, ε, ℵ, λ, π, τ, μ

\win\ + \shift\ + num:: results in ordinal notation...

       -with 'x'||'.'||'p'||'s'||'g'  				results in  ×, ·, ℿ, ⅀, ℾ
